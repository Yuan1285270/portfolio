## Design Notes

### Color Palette
- Primary: Modern tech blue/purple gradient
- Accent: Vibrant tech colors (cyan, lime)
- Background: Clean white or dark mode option
- Text: High contrast for readability

### Typography
- Headlines: Bold, modern sans-serif
- Body: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Code/Tech: Monospace for technical terms

### Animation Strategy
- Hero section: Staggered entrance animations
- Project cards: Hover lift and glow effects
- Scroll sections: Fade-in and slide-up on scroll
- Achievements: Counter animations
- Parallax: Subtle background movement
